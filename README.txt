INTRO

Welcome to "The Sky and Depths of Kobrade Hills", a 7-Day-Rogue-Like game developed with libtcod and bearlibterminal in python.

For any feedback, bugs, comments or questions feel free to email me under jan_rl@gmx.de or twitter @Nicol_Bolas. Happy to get in touch with you!

Download page for the game: https://jan-rl.itch.io/tsadokh


THE GAME

In the Hills of Kobrade you find a mysterious locked church. Maybe your newly aquired jumping boots can help you to find a way into it... and into the adventure taking you to the sky, the depths and back up again.

Features:
*ASCII-3D-roguelike adventure
*Procedurally generated hills, caves and chasms
*Visual clues for orientation
*Jumping as key mechanic
*Permalife (you cannot die)


CONTROLS

Look at descriptions by hovering the mouse.

Move by arrow-keys or num-pad (8 directions)
[g] grab
[i] inventory
[o] screen bigger
[p] screen smaller
[h] help
ESC cancel,quit
ENTER confirm
